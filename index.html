XM

A makeover.

XM stands for Xue Min, an all-girls dormitory in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Student Halls of Residence. Growing up, boys were always associated with blue, and girls with pink. The dormitory was heavily associated with pink. It was in the representative colour, it was in all promotional materials, merchandise, every poster. However, it didn’t represent the core values and gave people an inaccurate impression, so The 17th hall association wanted a makeover, a rebrand.


The promotion video.

The year book.

The posters throughout the year.

So we asked outselves:
1. What impression are we making now?
Honest opinion from a respondent: People only study and have no life. Girls gossiping in their room playing with makeup and perfume.
Keywords: Flowery, pink, girly, quiet
2. What impression do we want to make?
Keywords: Independent, clean, refreshing, comfort
3. How to make that impression?
A new logo and colour scheme was introduced and integrated into the hall's products, social media and promotion material.

  • Date: 2019
  • Category: Integrated Campaign

Bitter Mahjong

Hong Kong Style.

Inspired by a local horror TV series, these bitter melons are risking their life to play mahjong. They are dying, but they don't care. This piece brings viewers back to the old Hong Kong, when life wasn’t so great, when life was bitter. Although deprived of freedom and kept in cages, the bitter melons still laugh through the chaos.
This series functions as a storyboard for a short animation as well.

  • Date: 2018
  • Category: 60x50cm, Digital illustration and storyboard.

Acrobatic Troupe in Subdivided Flats

Flexibility.

Hong Kong’s extreme living conditions are very well known around the world. With the high population density and a lack of affordable housing options, a good portion of the population are forced to live in such restricting and limited dimensions. Despite the circumstances, its residents still survive and thrive. Their flexibility is truly fascinating. Therefore acrobats are used as a metaphor in this piece.

  • Date: 2018
  • Category: 30x145cm, Mixed Media. Stitching, drawing, mage transfer.

Always Ready

Nike

The Brief:

-Integrate the ”Just do it” tagline.
